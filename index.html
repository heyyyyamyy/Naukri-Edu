Learning & Development

In internet industry, the biggest threat is from daily disruptions and new innovations. Hence, we need to stay relevant & ahead of the competitors in the market and build a company which is in sync with the evolving technologies and emerging customer needs.

Considering this fast pace of organizational growth required, it is critical for our people to continuously scale up and take larger roles. They need to stay abreast and equipped with the latest knowledge and skills that are out there. We also believe that when we create a culture in which employees can continuously grow and develop them, both personally & professionally, it leads to greater employee engagement & productivity.

With these objectives, we have a host of learning & development offerings at Info Edge which are centered around 70: 20: 10 mix

  • 70% on-the-job training
  • 20% ongoing coaching and feedback
  • 10% through host of learning offerings
